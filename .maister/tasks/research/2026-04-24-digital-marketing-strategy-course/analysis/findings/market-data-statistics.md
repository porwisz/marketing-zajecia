# Digital Marketing Market Data & Statistics (2024-2025)
## For Postgraduate E-Marketing Strategy Course

**Research date**: April 2025
**Purpose**: Course materials demonstrating scale, trends, and strategic importance of digital marketing

---

## 1. GLOBAL INTERNET & MOBILE USAGE

### Internet Penetration
- **5.64 billion** global internet users in early 2025 (68.7% of world population)
- Growth of **144 million new users** in 12 months (+2.6% YoY)
- **2.57 billion people** (31.3%) remain offline — significant future growth potential
- Average daily internet usage: **6 hours 38 minutes** per person

*Source: DataReportal, Digital 2025 April Global Statshot — https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2025-april-global-statshot*

### Mobile Usage
- **5.81 billion mobile users** worldwide (70.7% of global population)
- Smartphones account for **87% of all mobile handsets**
- Monthly mobile data consumption: **21.6 GB per user**
- Global cellular traffic: **163+ billion GB monthly**
- Mobile represents **65.3% of digital ad spend** in 2024

*Source: DataReportal, Digital 2025 April Global Statshot — https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2025-april-global-statshot*

## 3. GLOBAL DIGITAL ADVERTISING

### Total Market Size
- Global total advertising spend: **~US$1.1 trillion** in 2024
- Digital channels account for **72.7%** of total ad investment (~**US$790 billion**)
- Year-over-year growth: **+7.3%** (+US$75 billion)
- Programmatic advertising: **82.4%** of all digital ad spending

*Source: DataReportal, Digital 2025 Global Overview — https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2025-global-overview-report*

### Digital vs. Traditional
- Digital advertising: ~**72.7%** of total global ad spend in 2024
- In the US market specifically: digital is **~80%** of total ad spending in 2025 (up from 75%)
- Mobile alone: **65%** of all digital ad spend

*Source: DataReportal (global) / Statista US Digital Advertising overview*

### Leading Platforms

**Google (Alphabet)**
- Advertising revenue: **$264.59 billion in 2024** (up from $237.86 billion in 2023)
- Year-over-year growth: **~11%**
- Controls **>84%** of global desktop search traffic
- Market share in digital advertising: **~25%** globally

*Source: Statista, Google advertising revenue — https://www.statista.com/statistics/266249/advertising-revenue-of-google/*

**Meta (Facebook + Instagram)**
- Facebook ad reach: **2.34 billion** users (early 2025)
- Instagram ad reach: **3 billion MAU**
- 28% of marketers cite Facebook as their highest ROI social platform; Instagram second at 22%

**TikTok**
- Ad reach: **1.99 billion** monthly users (18+)
- **31%** of brands include TikTok in influencer marketing plans (highest selection rate)
- Converts **43.8%** of engaged users into buyers
- TikTok ranked #1 platform in brand influencer partnership plans

**Amazon**
- Revenue: nearly **$638 billion** in global marketplace revenue
- Retail media network: **$58.79 billion** in 2025
- Growing digital ad revenue share (third largest ad platform in the US)

*Source: Shopify Global Ecommerce Statistics / Sprout Social — https://sproutsocial.com/insights/social-media-statistics/*

### Social Media Ad Spend Forecast
- Social media advertising projected to reach **$317.33 billion in 2026**
- Short-form video delivers the **highest B2B marketing ROI** (41% of marketers)

## 4. E-COMMERCE

### Global Market Size
- **$6.007 trillion** in global e-commerce sales in 2024 (+7.7% YoY)
- **$6.419 trillion** projected for 2025 (+6.8%)
- **$6.88 trillion** forecast for 2026
- E-commerce represents **23.5%** of global retail sales in 2025 (up from 19.9% in 2024)
- Projected to reach **22.5% of total retail** by 2028
- B2C market alone: **$5.2 trillion** in 2024, forecast to reach **$9.8 trillion by 2033**

*Source: Shopify, Global E-Commerce Sales blog — https://www.shopify.com/blog/global-ecommerce-sales*

### Mobile Commerce (M-Commerce)
- Mobile transactions reached **$2.07 trillion in 2024** (up from $1.71 trillion in 2023)
- **77% of e-commerce website visits** via mobile devices in 2025
- Smartphones generated **nearly 80%** of all retail website visits worldwide in 2025
- M-Commerce projected to reach **$2.4 trillion in 2026** (growing 9.5% annually)

*Source: Shopify Global E-Commerce Statistics / Statista Online Shopping*

### Consumer Behavior
- **75%+ of internet users** shopped online in 2024
- **56.2%** of internet users aged 16+ buy something online every week
- Average annual online spending per shopper: **US$1,127**
- **Digital wallets** drive **66%** of global e-commerce spending
- Gen Z: **76% discover products on social media**; **39% have purchased directly via social**

### Social Commerce
- Social commerce market: **$821 billion in 2025**
- On pace to surpass **$1 trillion by 2028**
- Social media platforms collectively drive **over 60% of product discovery** (vs. Google's 34.5%)

*Source: Shopify Global Ecommerce / DataReportal 2025*

### Key Player
- **Amazon**: ~$638 billion in global revenue — largest online marketplace by GMV

## 5. DIGITAL MARKETING CHANNEL PERFORMANCE

### Email Marketing
- Average ROI: **$36–$50 return for every $1 spent** (reported by 30% of marketing leaders)
- 35% of companies report **$10–$36 ROI per $1 spent**
- 5% achieve **over $50 ROI per $1 spent**
- Average revenue per recipient: **~$2.05**
- Average open rate: **~50%** (including Apple MPP-inflated figures); click-through ~**5%**
- Only **21% of marketing leaders** still don't measure email ROI (down from 36% in 2023)
- **93% of marketers** report personalization improves conversions; yet only 13% use advanced techniques

**Industry-specific email ROI:**
| Industry | ROI Ratio |
|----------|-----------|
| Retail/eCommerce | 45:1 |
| Marketing/PR Agencies | 42:1 |
| Software & Technology | 36:1 |
| Media/Publishing/Entertainment | 32:1 |

*Source: Litmus, State of Email 2025 / HubSpot Email Marketing Stats — https://www.litmus.com/blog/email-marketing-roi/*

### SEO & Organic Search
- **53%** of all trackable website traffic comes from organic search (BrightEdge)
- Organic listings receive **73%** of all search clicks vs. paid
- Top organic Google result averages **32% CTR** on desktop; **26.9%** on mobile
- First page top 3 results receive **68.7%** of all clicks
- Less than **1%** of users click to Google's second page
- Google controls **>90%** of global search engine market share
- Google handles approximately **~2 trillion searches annually**
- B2B companies generate **double the revenue** from organic search vs. other channels
- **90.63%** of pages receive zero organic traffic from Google (Ahrefs)

*Source: Search Engine Journal SEO Statistics / SEMRush SEO Statistics*

### Content Marketing
- **87%** of B2B brands achieved brand awareness through content marketing (last 12 months)
- **74%** generated leads; **49%** generated direct sales/revenue
- **89% of marketers** now use generative AI tools in content creation
- **62%** use AI for brainstorming; **44%** for drafting content
- **46%** expect content marketing budgets to increase in 2025
- Top B2B content channels: short articles (92%), organic social (89%), video (76%)
- **85%** of B2B marketers say LinkedIn delivers best content marketing value

*Source: Content Marketing Institute, 2024-2025 — https://www.contentmarketinginstitute.com/articles/content-marketing-statistics/*

### Influencer Marketing
- Global influencer marketing industry: **$24 billion in 2024** (up from $21.1 billion in 2023)
- Growth: from $16.4 billion in 2022 — **+46%** in two years
- Influencer advertising spend: projected **$35.09 billion in 2024** (+13.9% YoY)
- **84.8%** of brands report influencer marketing as effective
- **83.8%** say influencer campaigns yield higher-quality customers vs. other channels
- **94%** of organizations say influencer marketing outperforms traditional digital advertising
- **30%** of marketers report highest ROI via Instagram for influencer campaigns
- **85.8%** of brands now dedicate specific budgets to influencer marketing (vs. 37% in 2017)
- **66.3%** of brands manage influencer marketing entirely in-house
- Influencer marketing often yields **2x–3x returns** vs. traditional digital

*Source: Influencer Marketing Hub, 2024 Benchmark Report — https://influencermarketinghub.com/influencer-marketing-statistics/*

### Social Media Marketing
- **28%** of marketers cite Facebook as highest ROI social platform
- **85%** of B2B marketers report LinkedIn delivers highest ROI for B2B content
- Short-form social videos cited by **41%** of B2B marketers as highest ROI format
- **138.9 million** Instagram Reels watched every minute globally
- TikTok: **43.8%** of users convert to buyers after engagement
- For the first time: brands spending more on influencer marketing than on paid social/digital ads

## 6. POLAND-SPECIFIC DATA

### Internet & Population
- Total population: **38.4 million** (2025)
- Internet users: **34.5 million** (89.8% penetration) — among EU's higher rates
- Mobile connections: **53.7 million** (140% of population — many users have multiple SIMs)
- Fixed download speed: **178.10 Mbps** median (significant infrastructure investment)
- Mobile download speed: **69.09 Mbps** median

*Source: DataReportal, Digital 2025 Poland — https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2025-poland*

### Social Media in Poland
- Total social media users: **29.0 million** (75.6% of population)
- Growth: **+1.1 million** in 12 months (+3.9% YoY)

## 7. KEY TRENDS SHAPING DIGITAL MARKETING STRATEGY (2024-2025)

1. **AI integration is mainstream**: 89% of marketers use generative AI tools; AI adoption in influencer marketing at 63%
2. **Short-form video dominates ROI**: Highest returns across B2B and B2C, driven by TikTok/Reels/Shorts
3. **Social commerce is crossing the $1 trillion threshold**: Product discovery has shifted from search to social
4. **Mobile-first is non-negotiable**: 77-80% of e-commerce traffic is mobile; 65% of digital ads target mobile
5. **Influencer marketing overtook paid social**: First time brands spend more on influencers than traditional digital ads
6. **Email ROI remains king among owned channels**: $36-50 return per $1 spent — unmatched by most paid channels
7. **Organic search still drives 53% of traffic**: SEO ROI exceeds most paid channels for sustained investment
8. **Retail media networks emerging**: Amazon-led model reached $58.79B — a new major digital ad category
9. **Privacy changes reshape measurement**: 21% of marketers still don't measure email ROI (down from 36%) — tracking gaps are closing
10. **LinkedIn B2B dominance**: 85% of B2B marketers say LinkedIn is their highest-ROI platform
