## Source Quality Criteria

All sources used in the final research report must meet at least one of:
- Published in 2024 or 2025 (for data and case studies)
- Authored by a recognized authority in the field (for frameworks and pedagogy)
- Official company publication (annual reports, investor relations)
- Peer-reviewed or industry-body publication (IAB, Statista, eMarketer)

For Polish-language sources, Marketing przy Kawie, Puls Biznesu, and IAB Polska are treated as credible trade sources.
