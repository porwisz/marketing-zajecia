## Purpose

This document is the single authoritative reference for reproducing the Framework Hierarchy Diagram in all three of its slide variants. Any designer or instructor working in Keynote, PowerPoint, Canva, or equivalent tool must be able to construct each variant exactly from this specification with no additional context.

The diagram appears on three slides:

- **Slide 6** — Introduction anchor (all levels, equal weight)
- **Slide 46** — Post-lunch strategic pivot (foundation levels emphasized, operational levels faded)
- **Slide 77** — Synthesis close (all levels, annotated with exercise links)

---

## Overall Layout

- **Orientation**: Vertical stack, top to bottom, reading from most strategic (Brand Strategy) to most operational (RACE).
- **Slide placement**: Centered horizontally. Diagram occupies approximately 40% of slide width (in a 16:9 Keynote slide, approximately 560–640pt wide out of 1440pt total).
- **Vertical span**: Full usable height of slide with modest top and bottom padding (approximately 40pt each side).
- **Box count**: 5 boxes.
- **Box height**: All five boxes equal height (approximately 90–100pt each).
- **Gap between boxes**: Equal to approximately one-third of box height (approximately 30pt).
- **Arrows**: One arrow between each consecutive pair of boxes (four arrows total), vertically centered between the bottom edge of the upper box and the top edge of the lower box.

---

## Box Specifications

All box corners are slightly rounded (4–6pt radius). No drop shadow.

| Level | Polish Primary Label | Polish Sub-label | Fill Color Name | Fill Color Hex | Border | Font |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — Brand Strategy | Strategia Marki | "Kim jesteśmy?" (5–10+ lat) | Deep Blue | #1A3A5C | None (see variant rules) | White, Bold, 24pt minimum |
| 2 — Digital Strategy | Strategia Cyfrowa | "Jak technologia transformuje organizację?" (wieloletnia) | Medium Blue | #2D6A9F | None (see variant rules) | White, Bold, 24pt minimum |
| 3 — Digital Marketing Strategy | Strategia Marketingu Cyfrowego | "Jak przyciągamy, konwertujemy i utrzymujemy klientów?" (roczna) | Medium Teal | #1A7A8A | None (see variant rules) | White, Bold, 24pt minimum |
| 4 — SOSTAC | SOSTAC | "Framework Planowania" | Dark Green | #2D7A4F | None (see variant rules) | White, Bold, 24pt minimum |
| 5 — RACE | RACE | "Framework Egzekucji" | Charcoal | #3A3A3A | None (see variant rules) | White, Bold, 24pt minimum |

### Label Typography Details

- **Primary label** (e.g., "Strategia Marki"): Bold, 24pt minimum, white, vertically centered in the upper two-thirds of the box.
- **Sub-label** (e.g., `"Kim jesteśmy?" (5–10+ lat)`): Regular weight or italic, 14–16pt, white, placed directly below the primary label with approximately 6pt gap.
- Both label lines are horizontally centered within the box.

## Arrow Specification

- **Shape**: Straight vertical line with no arrowhead at the top and a filled chevron arrowhead at the bottom (pointing downward).
- **Line weight**: 2–3pt (use 2pt at smaller diagram scales; 3pt at full slide-width scales).
- **Line style**: Solid (no dashes).
- **Color**: Matches the fill color of the source box (the box above the arrow).
  - Arrow between Level 1 and Level 2: Deep Blue (#1A3A5C)
  - Arrow between Level 2 and Level 3: Medium Blue (#2D6A9F)
  - Arrow between Level 3 and Level 4: Medium Teal (#1A7A8A)
  - Arrow between Level 4 and Level 5: Dark Green (#2D7A4F)
- **Length**: Spans the full gap between the bottom edge of the upper box and the top edge of the lower box (approximately 30pt).
- **Horizontal position**: Centered on the box width.

## Variant 1: Slide 6 — Introduction (All Equal Weight)

**Context**: This is the first time students see the full hierarchy. The diagram serves as a day map. No level is emphasized over any other.

### Rendering Rules

- All five boxes: **full opacity (100%)**, full color as specified in the Box Specifications table above.
- All four arrows: **full opacity (100%)**, colors as specified.
- No borders added to any box.
- No callout annotations.

### Caption

Placed below the diagram, centered, in 18pt regular weight, white or dark grey (#333333) depending on slide background:

> **"Dziś będziemy poruszać się przez całą tę hierarchię — od dołu do góry."**

## Variant 2: Slide 46 — Post-Lunch Foundation Emphasis

**Context**: Students have learned SOSTAC and RACE in the morning. This variant shifts attention to the strategic foundation (Brand Strategy and Digital Strategy). The operational layers are deliberately de-emphasized to signal the pivot.

### Rendering Rules

**Emphasized levels (full opacity + border)**:

| Level | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Level 1 — Strategia Marki | Full opacity (100%), full fill color (#1A3A5C), **white border added: 3pt solid white** |
| Level 2 — Strategia Cyfrowa | Full opacity (100%), full fill color (#2D6A9F), **white border added: 3pt solid white** |

**Faded levels (30% opacity)**:

| Level | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Level 3 — Strategia Marketingu Cyfrowego | Reduced to **30% opacity** (the box fill, labels, and sub-labels all fade uniformly) |
| Level 4 — SOSTAC | Reduced to **30% opacity** |
| Level 5 — RACE | Reduced to **30% opacity** |

**Arrows**:

- Arrow between Level 1 and Level 2: full opacity (100%).
- Arrows between Levels 2–3, 3–4, and 4–5: reduced to **30% opacity** (matching the faded boxes).

**Implementation note**: In Keynote, apply opacity to the group of shapes (box + labels) for each faded level rather than adjusting individual fill alpha values. This ensures the text and border also fade together.

### Caption

Placed below the diagram, centered, 18pt:

> **"Zbudowaliśmy plan (SOSTAC) i mapę egzekucji (RACE). Teraz: fundament strategiczny."**

## Variant 3: Slide 77 — Synthesis (Annotated)

**Context**: All five frameworks have now been taught and practiced. The diagram is shown again at full weight, but each level is annotated with the exercise that applied it, completing the pedagogical loop for students.

### Rendering Rules

- All five boxes: **full opacity (100%)**, full color as specified (identical to Variant 1).
- All four arrows: **full opacity (100%)**, colors as specified.
- No borders added to any box.

### Callout Annotations

Each callout is positioned to the **right side** of its corresponding box. Format: a short horizontal bracket (or thin connector line) extends from the right edge of the box approximately 20pt, then a small text label is placed flush to the end of that bracket. The callout text is 13–14pt, non-bold, white or light grey (#CCCCCC) on a dark slide background, or dark grey (#333333) on a light slide background.

| Level | Callout Text |
|---|---|
| Level 1 — Strategia Marki | → Ćwiczenie 3: Kanwa Propozycji Wartości |
| Level 2 — Strategia Cyfrowa | (kontekst organizacyjny — omówiony w bloku 1) |
| Level 3 — Strategia Marketingu Cyfrowego | → Cały dzień: wybrana marka |
| Level 4 — SOSTAC | → Ćwiczenie 1: Audyt Sytuacyjny |
| Level 5 — RACE | → Ćwiczenie 2: Mapa Ścieżki Klienta |

**Layout note**: The callout column sits entirely within the slide boundaries. If the diagram is 40% of slide width and centered, the callouts occupy the space between the right edge of the diagram and the right margin of the slide (approximately 30% of slide width). Do not let callout text overlap the diagram boxes.

### Caption

Placed below the diagram, centered, 18pt:

> **"Teraz znacie całą hierarchię — i potraficie ją zastosować."**

## Do aktualizacji (co rok)

The diagram structure, labels, colors, and arrow specifications are **stable** — they do not require annual review. Update only the following if course content changes:

- **Callout text (Variant 3)**: If exercise names or numbering change, update the five callout labels in the Variant 3 table above.
- **Box sub-labels**: If the pedagogical framing of any level changes (e.g., the defining question for each level), update the Polish sub-label in the Box Specifications table.
- **Caption text**: If slide context changes (e.g., Slide 77 is renumbered), update the caption in the relevant variant section.

No color or shape changes are expected for annual cohort updates.
